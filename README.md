## Overview

This project applies data mining techniques to analyze telecom customer data and predict customer churn. The project combines **classification** and **clustering** algorithms to identify customers who are likely to leave the company and group customers with similar characteristics based on demographic information and service usage.

The project demonstrates how predictive analytics can help telecom companies improve customer retention and support better business decision-making.

---

## Project Objectives

- Predict customer churn (Yes / No) using classification techniques.
- Cluster customers based on demographic information and service usage.
- Analyze customer behavior through data mining methods.
- Compare the performance of different machine learning approaches.
- Support customer retention using predictive analytics.

---

## Project Motivation

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecommunications industry.

Retaining existing customers is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. By predicting customer churn, companies can proactively improve customer satisfaction through personalized offers, better services, and targeted marketing campaigns.

Customer clustering also provides valuable insights into customer segments, helping organizations develop more effective business strategies.
